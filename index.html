Sony

Sony Group Corporation is a global leader in electronics, entertainment, and gaming.
Founded in Japan in 1946, Sony creates everything from PlayStation consoles and cameras to music, movies, and financial services.
Known for innovation and creativity, Sony continues to shape the future of technology and storytelling.
